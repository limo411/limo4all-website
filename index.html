Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a limo in Toronto cost?
Rates start at $75 for an executive sedan for airport transfers. Corporate and hourly rates vary by service and duration. All rates are flat — locked at booking with no surge pricing ever. Get an instant quote online.
Are your rates truly all-inclusive?
Yes. Tolls, fuel surcharges, airport access fees, and gratuity are all included in the flat rate. The price you see at booking is the price you pay. No add-ons, no surprises at drop-off.
Do you track my flight if it is delayed?
Yes. We use live ADS-B flight data, not your scheduled arrival time. A two-hour delay automatically adjusts your driver at no extra charge. You do not need to call us.
What services do you offer?
We offer airport transfers (YYZ, YTZ, YHM, BUF), corporate travel, wedding limo, events and concerts, hourly charter, Niagara tours, Sprinter van group service, and city-to-city transfers.
How do I book a limo?
Book online using the form on this page and get an instant flat rate. You can also call our 24/7 dispatch team at 1-800-XXX-XXXX. Confirmation is sent by email and SMS within 60 seconds of booking.
Are your drivers licensed and background checked?
Yes. Every Limo4All chauffeur is criminal background checked, provincially licensed with full G licence, and trained to our professional standard. We do not use gig app drivers.
Which areas do you serve?
We serve all of the Greater Toronto Area including Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Vaughan, Markham, Oakville, Hamilton, Niagara Falls, and surrounding communities. City-to-city transfers available to Ottawa, Kingston, and more.
Do you offer wedding and event packages?
Yes. We offer dedicated wedding limo packages for the full wedding party, as well as event transfers for concerts, galas, and sporting events including FIFA 2026. Group pricing available for 4+ vehicles.
What vehicles do you have?
Our fleet includes executive sedans (Lincoln Continental / Mercedes E-Class), luxury SUVs (Cadillac Escalade / GMC Yukon), and Mercedes Sprinter vans for up to 14 passengers. All vehicles are fully insured and freshly detailed.
Can I cancel or change my booking?
Cancellations with more than 24 hours notice carry no fee. For changes, contact our dispatch team by phone, SMS, or email and we will accommodate when possible at no extra charge.
Do you provide car seats for children?
Yes. Request an infant or toddler seat at booking. No extra charge. Please specify your child’s age so we provide the correct seat type.
Can I book for a corporate account with monthly billing?
Yes. We offer consolidated monthly billing for corporate accounts. Travel coordinators can book on behalf of executives, and all receipts are provided digitally. Contact us to set up a corporate account.
