Airport Limo FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does an airport limo from Toronto cost?
Flat rate from $75 for a sedan and $95 for an SUV from downtown Toronto to Pearson. Mississauga starts at $80 sedan / $100 SUV. All rates locked at booking with no surge pricing ever.
Do you track my flight if it is delayed?
Yes. We use live flight data, not your scheduled arrival time. A two-hour delay adjusts your driver automatically at no extra charge. You do not need to call us.
How long will my driver wait at the airport?
60 minutes complimentary from actual landing time for international arrivals, 30 minutes for domestic. The clock starts when your wheels touch down, not the scheduled time.
Where exactly will my driver meet me at Pearson (YYZ)?
Inside the arrivals hall on Level 1 at Terminal 1 or Terminal 3, holding a personalized name board. No navigating outdoor pickup lanes or finding the car yourself.
Do you serve early morning flights at 2am, 3am, or 4am?
Yes. Our dispatch runs 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Early-morning and red-eye flights are among our most common bookings. Book 48 hours ahead for pre-dawn departures.
What if my flight is cancelled?
Call us as soon as you know. Cancellations with more than 2 hours notice carry no fee. We can rebook you for the next available flight at no additional charge.
Which airports do you serve?
Toronto Pearson (YYZ), Billy Bishop (YTZ), Hamilton John C. Munro (YHM), Niagara Falls International, and Buffalo Niagara International (BUF) for cross-border transfers.
Is the flat rate truly all-inclusive?
Yes. Tolls, fuel surcharges, airport access fees, and gratuity are all included. The rate you see at booking is what you pay. No add-ons at drop-off.
Can I book for someone else — a family member or client?
Absolutely. Many corporate bookings are made by travel coordinators for executives. Provide the passenger’s name and flight number and we track their arrival directly.
Do you offer car seats for infants or toddlers?
Yes. Request an infant or toddler seat at booking. No extra charge. Please specify the child’s age so we can provide the correct seat type.
How early should I leave for the airport from downtown Toronto?
Minimum 2 hours before domestic departures and 3 hours before international. We factor in current traffic and will alert you if conditions require an earlier departure.
Do you charge extra for oversized luggage or ski equipment?
Sedans accommodate 3 standard bags. For oversized items such as skis or golf bags, we recommend the SUV or Sprinter. No extra charge when booked with the appropriate vehicle.
