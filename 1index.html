Frequently Asked Questions

How much does limo service cost in Toronto?
Our flat rates start from $65 for a sedan to Toronto Pearson (YYZ). Prices vary by vehicle type and destination. Use our price checker above or call 1-800-123-4567 for an instant quote.
Do you track my flight for delays?
Yes. We monitor all incoming flights in real time. If your flight is delayed, your driver automatically adjusts pickup time at no extra charge. You will not be waiting at the airport.
How early should I book?
We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance. For peak times, holidays and early morning flights, 48 to 72 hours notice is ideal. Last-minute bookings are accommodated subject to availability.
What is included in the flat rate?
All flat rates include meet and greet, waiting time (60 min international, 30 min domestic), luggage assistance and all taxes. No hidden fees or surge pricing ever.
What areas of the GTA do you serve?
We serve all of the GTA including Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Markham, Richmond Hill, Vaughan, Oakville, Burlington, Hamilton and beyond. See our full coverage above.
